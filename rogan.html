Rogan Josh Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (bone-in preferred)
  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger paste
  • 1 tbsp garlic paste
  • 2–3 dried red chilies (soaked and ground to a paste)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp fennel powder
  • 1/2 tsp dry ginger powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri red chili powder (for color)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 4 green cardamoms, 4 cloves, 1 black cardamom, 1 small cinnamon stick

Instructions

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a heavy-bottomed pan and add whole spices. Sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté till the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add red chili paste and turmeric. Cook for 1-2 mins.
  5. Add mutton pieces and cook on high heat for 5–6 mins until sealed.
  6. Lower heat and add yogurt gradually, stirring continuously.
  7. Add fennel powder, dry ginger powder, Kashmiri chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Cover and simmer on low heat for 45–60 minutes until meat is tender. Add water if needed.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala, cook for another 5 mins, and turn off heat.
  10. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan.
