Masala Papad Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 4 roasted papads (urad dal or moong dal)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Dry roast or microwave the papads until crisp. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix onion, tomato, green chili, coriander, chaat masala, chili powder, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Just before serving, spread the masala mixture over the roasted papads.
  4. Serve immediately to maintain crispiness.
