Dahi Kebab Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hung curd (thick dahi)
  • 1/4 cup grated paneer
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 tsp garam masala
  • 2-3 tbsp bread crumbs (for binding)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix hung curd, grated paneer, onions, coriander, chilies, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt.
  2. Add bread crumbs and mix until the mixture holds shape. Adjust if too sticky.
  3. Shape the mixture into small round kebabs.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry kebabs till golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or sauce.
