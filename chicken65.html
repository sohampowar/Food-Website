Chicken 65 Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into small cubes)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • Few curry leaves
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with egg, corn flour, maida, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, salt, and lemon juice. Mix well and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil and deep fry chicken pieces until golden and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towel.
  3. In a separate pan, heat a little oil. Add curry leaves and green chilies, sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add the fried chicken and toss well for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
  5. Serve hot as a starter or with onion rings and lemon wedges.
