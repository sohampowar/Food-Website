Aloo Paratha Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour (for dough)
  • Water as needed to knead
  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Ghee or oil for roasting

Instructions

  1. Knead a soft dough using wheat flour and water. Rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes with spices, green chilies, ginger, coriander, and salt.
  3. Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll one ball into a small circle, place the potato filling in the center.
  4. Seal the edges and gently roll into a paratha.
  5. Heat a tawa and roast the paratha on both sides with ghee or oil until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with curd, butter, or pickle.
