Veg Fried Rice Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped beans
  • 1/2 cup chopped capsicum
  • 1/2 cup chopped cabbage
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a wok on high flame.
  2. Add all chopped vegetables and saut√© for 3-4 minutes until slightly tender yet crisp.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix gently.
  4. Sprinkle soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and black pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes on high flame.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
