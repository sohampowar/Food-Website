Egg Curry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 4–6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté cumin seeds.
  2. Add chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook for 5–6 minutes until oil starts to separate.
  5. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add 1/2 cup water and bring the curry to a boil.
  7. Gently add the boiled eggs (you can make slits on them to absorb flavor).
  8. Simmer for 5–7 minutes on low heat.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with rice or roti.
