Malai Kofta Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • For Kofta:
    • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
    • 1/2 cup grated paneer
    • 2 tbsp corn flour
    • Salt to taste
    • Chopped coriander
    • Oil for frying
  • For Gravy:
    • 2 tbsp oil or butter
    • 1 onion (chopped)
    • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
    • 10 cashews (soaked and ground)
    • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • 1/2 tsp garam masala
    • 1/4 cup fresh cream
    • Salt to taste
    • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, paneer, corn flour, salt, and coriander. Shape into balls.
  2. Fry the balls until golden and set aside on paper towel.
  3. For gravy, heat oil, saut√© onions till golden, add ginger-garlic paste, cook well.
  4. Add tomato puree, cashew paste, turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt.
  5. Cook until oil separates, then add cream and mix well.
  6. Add water to adjust consistency. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Before serving, place koftas in the gravy and top with garam masala and cream.
  8. Serve hot with naan, paratha, or jeera rice.
