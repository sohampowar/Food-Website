Palak Paneer Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach (palak) leaves
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp cream or fresh malai
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach leaves in boiling water for 2–3 minutes, then transfer to cold water. Blend into a smooth puree with green chilies.
  2. In a pan, heat oil/ghee and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until raw smell goes away.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the spinach puree and cook for 5–7 minutes on medium flame.
  8. Add paneer cubes and simmer for another 5 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and add cream. Stir gently.
  10. Serve hot with roti, naan, or jeera rice.
