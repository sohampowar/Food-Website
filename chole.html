Chole Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with water and a pinch of salt until soft (4–5 whistles).
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf. Let them sizzle.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add cooked chickpeas along with some of the cooking water.
  8. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until flavors are well blended.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and chole masala. Mix and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  10. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with bhature, poori, or rice.
