Bhindi Masala Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 250g bhindi (okra), chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry bhindi completely before chopping to avoid stickiness.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and sauté bhindi until lightly crisp. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the sautéed bhindi and mix to coat with masala.
  8. Cover and cook on low heat for 5–6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with roti or paratha.
