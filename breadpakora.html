Bread Pakora Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 6 bread slices
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Water as needed (for batter)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mash boiled potatoes and mix with green chili, ginger, cumin, garam masala, chili powder, salt, and coriander.
  2. Cut bread slices in half diagonally. Stuff the potato mixture between two halves if making stuffed pakoras.
  3. In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric, salt, and enough water to make a smooth batter.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan for frying.
  5. Dip each bread (or stuffed bread) into the batter and coat evenly.
  6. Carefully drop into hot oil and fry till golden and crisp on both sides.
  7. Drain on tissue paper and serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
