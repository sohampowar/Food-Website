Pakora Platter Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sliced onions
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup grated cabbage
  • 1 boiled & mashed potato
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp carom seeds (ajwain)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1.5 cups besan (gram flour)
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix all vegetables in a bowl along with green chilies, ginger, and spices.
  2. Add besan and mix well. Add water slowly to make a thick batter.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil.
  4. Fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain excess oil on paper towels.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or tomato ketchup.
