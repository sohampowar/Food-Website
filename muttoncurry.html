Mutton Curry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom
  • 2 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and ghee in a pressure cooker. Add whole spices and sauté for a few seconds.
  2. Add onions and fry until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell goes away.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add mutton pieces and cook on high flame until sealed and browned.
  7. Add yogurt and mix well. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
  8. Add 1 to 1.5 cups of water. Pressure cook for 4-5 whistles or until mutton is tender.
  9. Open the lid, simmer if needed. Add garam masala and chopped coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with rice or roti.
