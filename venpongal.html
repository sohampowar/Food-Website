Ven Pongal Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal (yellow split)
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp cashew nuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 3.5 to 4 cups water

Instructions

  1. Dry roast the moong dal lightly. Rinse rice and dal together.
  2. In a pressure cooker, add rice, dal, water, and salt. Cook for 3-4 whistles until soft.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan. Add cashews and fry till golden. Remove and keep aside.
  4. In the same ghee, add cumin, pepper, ginger, and curry leaves. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  5. Add this tempering and cashews to the cooked pongal. Mix well.
  6. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
