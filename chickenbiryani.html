Chicken Biryani Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (with bone)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 minutes)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp biryani masala (optional)
  • Few strands saffron (soaked in milk)
  • 2 tbsp oil + 1 tbsp ghee
  • Whole spices: bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  2. Add whole spices, ginger-garlic paste, tomato, and cook well.
  3. Add chicken, yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and cook until chicken is 80% done.
  4. Boil rice with salt and whole spices until 70% cooked. Drain water.
  5. In a large pot, layer chicken masala and partially cooked rice. Sprinkle fried onions, coriander, mint, and saffron milk.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Mix gently and serve hot with raita or salad.
