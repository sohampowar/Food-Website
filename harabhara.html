Hara Bhara Kabab Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 cup blanched spinach (palak)
  • 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander and mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornflour or breadcrumbs (for binding)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Blend spinach, peas, mint, coriander, and green chili into a coarse paste.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash boiled potatoes and add the green paste.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, amchur, salt, and cornflour. Mix well to form a dough.
  4. Divide into equal portions and shape into kababs.
  5. Heat oil on a tawa and shallow fry kababs until golden and crisp on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
