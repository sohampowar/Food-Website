Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper

Preparation

  1. Boil the pasta until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta, pancetta, and egg mix.
  5. Serve hot with extra cheese.
