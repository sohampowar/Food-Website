Keema Matar Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g minced mutton/chicken
  • 1 cup green peas (matar)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté till raw smell goes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till they are soft.
  5. Add turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add minced meat and cook on high heat for 5-6 minutes, stirring constantly.
  7. Lower heat, cover and cook until the meat is tender (15–20 mins). Add little water if needed.
  8. Add green peas and cook for another 5–7 minutes.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
