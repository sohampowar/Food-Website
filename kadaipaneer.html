Kadai Paneer Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 capsicum (sliced)
  • 1 onion (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds (crushed)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala or kadai masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and crushed coriander seeds.
  2. Add onions and capsicum, sauté until slightly soft but still crunchy.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add paneer cubes and toss gently to coat with the masala.
  7. Cook on low flame for 5–7 minutes until flavors are absorbed.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala or kadai masala and stir lightly.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
  10. Enjoy with naan, roti, or jeera rice.
