Aloo Tikki Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 4 large boiled potatoes
  • 1/4 cup boiled green peas
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 2 chopped green chilies
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornflour or breadcrumbs (for binding)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Mash the boiled potatoes and peas in a large bowl.
  2. Add green chilies, ginger, coriander, and all the dry spices. Mix well.
  3. Add cornflour or breadcrumbs for binding. Mix and shape into flat tikkis.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the tikkis on medium flame until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind chutney.
