Tandoori Chicken Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil or vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Red food color (optional)

Instructions

  1. Make deep cuts on the chicken pieces for better marination.
  2. In a bowl, mix curd, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, oil, and food color.
  3. Apply the marinade to the chicken thoroughly and let it rest for at least 2–4 hours or overnight in the fridge.
  4. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F) or prepare a grill/pan.
  5. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray or grill rack.
  6. Cook for 25–30 minutes, turning once halfway until fully cooked and slightly charred.
  7. Brush with a little butter or oil for extra flavor before serving.
  8. Serve hot with mint chutney, lemon wedges, and onion rings.
