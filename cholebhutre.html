Chole Bhature Recipe
Back

Ingredients

For Chole (Chickpeas)

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

For Bhature

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed to knead
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

Chole

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt and water for 4-5 whistles or until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a minute.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, and chole masala. Cook till oil separates.
  5. Add the boiled chickpeas and mix well. Add water if needed. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Bhature

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, semolina, salt, sugar, baking soda, and curd.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft dough. Cover and rest for 2-3 hours.
  3. Divide dough into small balls and roll into discs.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry bhature one by one until puffed and golden.
  5. Serve hot with chole, onions, and pickle.
