Matar Paneer Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© till golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook till soft and mushy.
  5. Add turmeric, chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add green peas and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add paneer cubes and a little water to make a gravy. Mix well.
  8. Cover and simmer for 5-10 minutes until peas are soft and flavors are blended.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
