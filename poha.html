Poha Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp peanuts
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Lemon juice (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Rinse poha in a strainer under running water until soft. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and peanuts. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add chopped onions and cook till they turn translucent.
  5. Add turmeric powder and mix well.
  6. Add soaked poha, salt, and sugar. Mix gently and cook for 2-3 minutes on low flame.
  7. Turn off heat and add lemon juice. Mix gently.
  8. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot.
