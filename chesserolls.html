Cheese Balls Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 cup grated mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup water (to make slurry)
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix mashed potatoes, grated cheese, chili flakes, pepper, salt, coriander, and cornflour.
  2. Shape into small round balls.
  3. Make a slurry using flour and water.
  4. Dip each ball into the slurry, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Refrigerate for 15â€“20 minutes to firm up.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with ketchup or spicy mayo.
