Methi Thepla Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup fresh fenugreek (methi) leaves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tsp oil (for dough)
  • Water as required for kneading
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix wheat flour, chopped methi, curd, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and all dry spices.
  2. Add oil and water gradually to knead a soft dough. Let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into equal balls and roll each into a thin round thepla.
  4. Heat a tawa and cook each thepla using oil or ghee till golden spots appear on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with pickle, curd, or tea.
