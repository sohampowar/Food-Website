Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal into a smooth batter. Grind rice into a slightly coarse paste.
  3. Mix both batters, add salt, and allow it to ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  4. Heat a dosa tawa or flat pan. Grease lightly and pour a ladleful of batter.
  5. Spread in circular motion to form a thin dosa. Drizzle some oil around edges.
  6. Cook until the bottom turns golden and crispy. Flip if needed (optional).
  7. Serve hot with chutney, sambar, or potato masala.
