Misal Pav Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted moth beans (matki)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp goda masala or garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Farsan (for topping)
  • Chopped coriander and onion (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook till soft.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili powder, and masala. Mix well.
  5. Add sprouted matki, salt, and water. Cover and cook until beans are soft.
  6. Adjust consistency. It should be a bit watery (called "rassa").
  7. To serve, pour misal in a bowl, top with farsan, chopped onion, coriander, and lemon juice.
  8. Serve hot with pav and enjoy!
