Chilli Paneer Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tbsp chopped ginger
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers (green/red)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1/4 tsp sugar
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix paneer cubes with all-purpose flour, cornflour, salt, and pepper. Sprinkle water to coat lightly.
  2. Deep fry or shallow fry paneer until golden and crisp. Set aside.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add garlic, ginger, and green chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Add onions and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2 minutes on high flame.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, vinegar, and sugar. Mix well.
  6. Add the fried paneer cubes. Toss gently to coat paneer with the sauce.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
