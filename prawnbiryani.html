Prawn Biryani Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp biryani masala or garam masala
  • Chopped coriander and mint leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Cook basmati rice until 80% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Marinate prawns with turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Rest for 15 minutes.
  3. In a pan, heat oil/ghee, add whole spices and sliced onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  5. Add curd, biryani masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add prawns and cook for 5-6 minutes until just done.
  7. Layer the rice over the prawns masala. Top with saffron milk, mint, and coriander leaves.
  8. Cover tightly and cook on dum (low flame) for 15–20 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with raita or salad.
