Onion Rings Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, cut into thick rings
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water (as needed for batter)
  • Breadcrumbs (for coating)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Separate the onion slices into rings and keep aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix all-purpose flour, cornflour, baking powder, salt, pepper, and chili powder.
  3. Add cold water gradually to form a thick but smooth batter.
  4. Dip each onion ring into the batter, then coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the onion rings in batches until golden and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with ketchup or mayo dip.
