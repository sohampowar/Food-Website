Spring Rolls Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 12 spring roll sheets
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup grated carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped spring onions
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil for saut√©ing
  • Oil for deep frying
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose flour + 2 tbsp water (for sealing)

Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add spring onions, carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add soy sauce, vinegar, pepper, and salt. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes. Let the filling cool.
  3. Place a spoonful of filling on a spring roll sheet. Fold and roll tightly, sealing the edges with the flour-water paste.
  4. Repeat for all sheets.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry spring rolls until golden and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with chili sauce or ketchup.
