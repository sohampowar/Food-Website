Moong Dal Chilla Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yellow moong dal (soaked 2-3 hours)
  • 1 green chili
  • 1-inch ginger piece
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 small onion (optional), finely chopped
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Grind soaked moong dal, green chili, and ginger into a smooth batter using little water.
  2. Transfer batter to a bowl. Add salt, turmeric, cumin seeds, coriander, and onion if using. Mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick tawa or pan and grease lightly with oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread into a round chilla.
  5. Drizzle some oil around it and cook on medium flame till golden and crispy.
  6. Flip and cook the other side till done.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or curd.
