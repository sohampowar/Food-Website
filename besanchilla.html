Besan Chilla Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, onion, green chili, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to make a smooth batter of pouring consistency.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan or tawa and grease lightly with oil.
  4. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread gently into a round shape.
  5. Drizzle some oil around the chilla and cook on medium flame until golden.
  6. Flip and cook the other side till done.
  7. Serve hot with chutney or curd.
