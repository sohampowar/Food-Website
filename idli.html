Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split & dehusked black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed for soaking and grinding
  • Oil or ghee for greasing idli plates

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak idli rice and urad dal separately with fenugreek seeds for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind urad dal into a smooth, fluffy batter. Grind rice to a slightly coarse paste.
  3. Mix both batters well, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8-12 hours.
  4. After fermentation, stir the batter gently.
  5. Grease idli plates with oil or ghee and pour the batter into molds.
  6. Steam idlis in a steamer or pressure cooker (without whistle) for 10-12 minutes.

  8. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
