Methi Malai Matar Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup green peas (boiled or frozen)
  • 1 cup fresh methi leaves (fenugreek), chopped
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional)
  • 1/2 inch ginger
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup cashews (soaked)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream or malai
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee

Instructions

  1. Grind onion, green chili, ginger, garlic, and soaked cashews to a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté until they splutter.
  3. Add the paste and sauté on medium heat until oil separates.
  4. Add chopped methi leaves and sauté for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add green peas, turmeric, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add cream and little water to adjust consistency.
  7. Simmer for 5–6 minutes until everything blends well.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  9. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or rice.
