Fish Fry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets (any firm fish, cleaned)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp rice flour (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions

  1. Clean and pat dry the fish pieces.
  2. In a bowl, mix lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, and all the spices.
  3. Apply the marinade to the fish. Rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Coat marinated fish lightly with rice flour.
  5. Heat oil in a pan for shallow frying.
  6. Place fish pieces in the hot oil and fry on medium heat.
  7. Cook each side for 3-4 minutes or until golden brown and crisp.
  8. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with onion rings, lemon wedges, and mint chutney.
