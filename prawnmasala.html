Prawn Masala Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped or pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2–3 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  4. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander, cumin powder, and salt. Mix well and cook the masala for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Add prawns and cook on medium heat until they turn pink and are cooked through (around 5–6 minutes).
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice, chapati, or paratha.
