Uttapam Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 medium tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot (optional)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp salt (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa on medium flame. Grease lightly with oil or ghee.
  2. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter in the center and spread slightly to make a thick round uttapam.
  3. Sprinkle chopped onions, tomatoes, green chili, grated carrot, and coriander evenly on top.
  4. Press the toppings gently with a spatula so they stick to the batter.
  5. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges and cook until the base is golden brown.
  6. Flip carefully and cook the other side for 1â€“2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
