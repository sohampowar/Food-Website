Corn Fritters Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sweet corn (boiled)
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 2 tbsp rice flour
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Crush boiled corn lightly using a mixer or blender.
  2. In a bowl, combine crushed corn, onion, chilies, coriander, besan, rice flour, cumin, and salt.
  3. Add little water to make a thick batter consistency.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan. Drop spoonfuls of the mixture into the oil.
  5. Fry till golden brown and crispy on medium flame.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve hot with chutney or sauce.
