Veg Biryani Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1.5 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20–30 minutes. Cook until 90% done and drain.
  2. In a pan, heat oil/ghee and sauté whole spices.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add turmeric, chili powder, biryani masala, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add vegetables and sauté for a few minutes. Then add yogurt and cook until veggies are tender.
  8. Layer cooked rice on top, sprinkle coriander and mint leaves. Cover and dum cook for 10–15 mins on low heat.
  9. Serve hot with raita or salad.
