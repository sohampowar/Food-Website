Puri Bhaji Recipe
Back

Ingredients

For Puri

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp oil
  • Water as required (to knead)
  • Oil for deep frying

For Bhaji (Potato Curry)

  • 4 boiled potatoes (peeled & mashed)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1/2 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions

Puri

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and 1 tsp oil.
  2. Add water little by little and knead a stiff dough.
  3. Divide into small balls and roll into small puris.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry puris till golden and puffed.
  5. Remove on paper towel to drain excess oil.

Bhaji

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, let them splutter.
  2. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until soft and translucent.
  4. Add turmeric, ginger, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add mashed potatoes and about 1/2 cup water. Mix and simmer.
  6. Cook for 5–7 minutes until everything blends well.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with puris.
