Vegetable Kurma Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • Whole spices (1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 1 inch cinnamon, 2 cardamom)

To Grind

  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1 tsp poppy seeds (optional)
  • 1 tsp fennel seeds
  • 5-6 cashews
  • 2 green chilies
  • Water (as needed for grinding)

Instructions

  1. Grind all the "To Grind" ingredients to a smooth paste and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add whole spices and let them sizzle.
  3. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté for a couple of minutes.
  8. Add water to cover the veggies. Cover and cook until vegetables are soft.
  9. Once cooked, add the ground coconut paste and mix well.
  10. Simmer for 5–7 minutes until the kurma thickens and flavors blend.
  11. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with chapati, dosa, or puri.
