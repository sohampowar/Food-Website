Chilli Chicken Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp corn flour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 onion (cut into cubes)
  • 1 green capsicum (cut into cubes)
  • 2â€“3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with egg, corn flour, maida, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper. Let it rest for 30 mins.
  2. Deep fry marinated chicken until golden and crispy. Set aside.
  3. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a wok. Add green chilies, onions, and capsicum. Stir-fry on high flame.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, vinegar, sugar, and mix well.
  5. Add fried chicken pieces and toss well to coat with sauce.
  6. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot as a starter or with fried rice.
