Navratan Korma Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potatoes, bell peppers)
  • 1/4 cup paneer cubes
  • 1/4 cup pineapple cubes (optional, for sweet version)
  • 1 tbsp raisins
  • 1 tbsp chopped cashews
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Whole spices: 1 bay leaf, 2 cloves, 1-inch cinnamon, 2 cardamoms
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Chopped coriander for garnish

To Grind (Make Paste)

  • 1/4 cup cashews
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp poppy seeds or melon seeds
  • Water (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Boil the mixed vegetables until just tender. Set aside.
  2. Grind the ingredients under "To Grind" to a smooth paste.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add whole spices and let them sizzle.
  4. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  6. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  7. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  8. Add the ground paste and cook for 3–4 minutes on low flame.
  9. Add boiled vegetables, paneer, raisins, and cashews. Mix gently.
  10. Add water to adjust the consistency. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  11. Add pineapple (if using) and cream. Mix and cook for 2 more minutes.
  12. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with coriander. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
