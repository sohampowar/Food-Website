Paneer Butter Masala Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Pour in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream and garam masala. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
