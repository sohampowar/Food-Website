Sabudana Khichdi Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sabudana (sago pearls)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled & diced
  • 1/2 cup roasted peanuts (crushed)
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt to taste (sendha namak for fasting)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • Lemon juice to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse sabudana 2-3 times and soak in water (just enough to cover it) for 5-6 hours or overnight.
  2. Drain excess water and fluff sabudana with a fork.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds and green chilies, and sauté.
  4. Add diced potatoes and sauté till lightly golden.
  5. Add sabudana and crushed peanuts. Mix gently on low flame.
  6. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, till sabudana turns translucent.
  7. Add salt, chopped coriander, and lemon juice. Mix well.
  8. Serve hot with curd or enjoy as is!
