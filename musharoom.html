Mushroom Pepper Fry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp black pepper (freshly crushed)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add sliced onions and sauté until translucent.
  2. Add green chilies and ginger-garlic paste. Sauté until raw smell disappears.
  3. Add mushrooms, turmeric powder, salt, and cook for 5–6 minutes until the mushrooms shrink and release water.
  4. Stir in crushed pepper and garam masala. Cook on high flame for 2–3 minutes until dry.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot as a starter or with rice.
