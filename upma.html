Upma Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rava (semolina/sooji)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1-inch ginger, grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 2.5 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Optional: Cashews, vegetables (carrot, peas)

Instructions

  1. Dry roast rava in a pan until aromatic. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal, and fry till golden.
  3. Add chopped chilies, ginger, curry leaves, and onions. Saut√© till onions are soft.
  4. Add water and salt. Bring it to a boil.
  5. Slowly add roasted rava while stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  6. Cook on low flame until water is absorbed and upma becomes soft and fluffy.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with chutney or pickle.
