Samosa Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp oil (for dough)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed to knead)
  • 3-4 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp oil (for filling)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and 4 tbsp oil. Add water gradually and knead into a stiff dough. Cover and rest for 30 mins.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and ginger. Saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add mashed potatoes, peas, spices, and salt. Mix well and cook for 3-4 minutes. Let it cool.
  4. Divide dough into small balls. Roll each into an oval, cut in half, and form a cone.
  5. Fill cone with potato mixture, seal the edges with water, and shape into a samosa.
  6. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry samosas on low-medium heat until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with tamarind or mint chutney.
