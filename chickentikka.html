Chicken Tikka Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chaat masala (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh coriander leaves & lemon wedges (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, lemon juice, oil, and salt.
  2. Add chicken pieces and coat them well. Marinate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight in the fridge).
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C (392°F). Place marinated chicken on skewers or baking tray.
  4. Grill or bake for 20–25 minutes, turning once halfway, until chicken is cooked and slightly charred.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala and garnish with coriander and lemon wedges.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney and onion rings.
