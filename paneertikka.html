Paneer Tikka Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup diced capsicum (bell peppers)
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • Oil or butter for cooking
  • Skewers (wooden or metal)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix curd, ginger-garlic paste, all spices, lemon juice, mustard oil, and salt to prepare marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes, capsicum, and onions. Coat well and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or more for best results).
  3. Thread the marinated paneer and veggies onto skewers.
  4. Heat a grill pan or tawa. Grease with oil/butter and place the skewers.
  5. Cook on medium flame, turning sides until charred and cooked evenly.
  6. Garnish with lemon wedges and onion rings. Serve hot with mint chutney.
