Baingan Bharta Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant (baingan)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 2–3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Roast the eggplant on an open flame or in the oven until the skin is charred and inside is soft.
  2. Cool and peel the skin. Mash the pulp and set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for a minute.
  6. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil separates.
  7. Add mashed eggplant and mix well. Cook for 5–7 minutes on low flame, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add garam masala and chopped coriander. Mix well.
  9. Serve hot with roti, paratha, or naan.
