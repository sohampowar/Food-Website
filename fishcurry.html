Fish Curry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g fish (cleaned and cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed or chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Mix well.
  6. Add water to make a gravy consistency. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Gently add fish pieces. Cover and cook on low heat for 8–10 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with rice or roti.
