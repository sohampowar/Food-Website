Mozzarella Sticks Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 200g mozzarella cheese (cut into sticks)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1.5 cups breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Cut mozzarella into thick sticks and freeze for 30 minutes to firm up.
  2. Prepare a breading station: flour in one bowl, beaten eggs in another, and breadcrumbs mixed with garlic powder, chili flakes, and salt in a third.
  3. Coat each cheese stick in flour, dip in egg, and coat with breadcrumbs. Repeat the egg and breadcrumb coating for a double crust.
  4. Freeze the coated sticks for at least 1 hour to avoid cheese melting while frying.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and fry the sticks until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve hot with marinara sauce or ketchup.
