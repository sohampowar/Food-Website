Aloo Gobi Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes (peeled & cubed)
  • 2 cups cauliflower florets
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add potatoes and cauliflower. Mix to coat with the masala.
  7. Cover and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until vegetables are tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and fresh coriander leaves. Mix gently.
  9. Serve hot with roti or paratha.
