Butter Chicken Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into cubes)
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, and salt. Rest for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan. Cook marinated chicken until lightly browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add remaining butter and tomato puree. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add cream, kasuri methi, and a little salt. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cooked chicken pieces and simmer for 10 more minutes, allowing flavors to blend.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
