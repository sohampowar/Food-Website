Chicken Curry Recipe
Back

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cleaned and cut)
  • 2 onions (finely sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish
  • 1 cup water (adjust for consistency)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry till the raw smell goes.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  5. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  6. Add chicken pieces and sauté for 5-6 minutes.
  7. Add water, cover, and cook until the chicken is tender.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and simmer for a few more minutes.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with rice or roti.
